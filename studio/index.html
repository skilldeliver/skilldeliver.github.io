Who is hacker? - Hacker ≠ security hacker. Hacker is a person who uses computer programming or technical skills to overcome a challenge or problem. Keywords to spot a good hacker - Linux, Vim, Emacs, Clojure, DFS.

Who is creative? - Creative is a person whose job involves original & artistic work. Some positions: graphic designer, UI/UX designer, motion designer, marketing specialist. Keywords to spot a good creative: Illustrator, DJI drone, Webflow, Grid, Conflunce/Notion.
